Arthur Silva Dantas received several outstanding awards throughout elementary school and, in particular, through science fairs, after developing research projects, especially during high school. In 2024, one of his greatest achievements was being accepted at the Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul, through call for applications No. 40/2024 - PROGRAD/UFMS.
